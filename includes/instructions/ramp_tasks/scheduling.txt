<h1>Presentation of tasks</h1>

<p>At the top right of the screen, the participant has a scheduler, which indicates for each task (represented by a capital letter and a timeline), the phases during which it will be active.</p>

<p>There is also an automation system capable of sharing the workload with the participant. When automation is active for a given task, the scheduler represents it.</p>

<p>This helps the participant manage their effort and allocate resources effectively.</p>
