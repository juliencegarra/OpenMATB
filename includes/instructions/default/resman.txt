<h1>Resource Management</h1>

<p>In this task, you must control the leakage of the two main reservoirs (A and B) using a set of backup reservoirs and pumps, each with its own specific power.</p>

<p>Your task is to turn on/off the numbered pumps to bring reservoirs A and B as close as possible to their normal levels (between the black markers on the reservoirs).</p>

<p>Please familiarise yourself with the location of keys 1 to 6 on the numpad. </p>
