<h1>Communications (Bottom Left)</h1>

<p>In this task, you must repeat the frequency of the channel when your call sign is signaled. </p>

<p>If a message is directed to you: Press and hold the trigger button or the space key while repeating the message.</p>

<p>If a message is directed to another call sign: Ignore it.</p>

<p>Your call sign is displayed at the top of the communications task window. Always hold the trigger button or space key down while responding.</p>
