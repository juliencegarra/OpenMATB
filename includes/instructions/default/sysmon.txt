<h1>System monitoring (Top Left)</h1>

<p>In this task, you are incharge of monitoring a set of <strong>six gauges</strong>.</p>

<p>You must correct the system if necessary: when the green light goes off (F5), when the red light turns on (F6), or when any of the moving arrows reaches the end of one of the four scales (F1 to F4).</p>

<p>Please familiarise yourself with the location of the F1-F6 keys before you proceed.</p>
